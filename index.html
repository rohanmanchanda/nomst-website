Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI-powered arbitration services.

Is Nomst's arbitration legally binding?

Yes. Our arbitration decisions carry the same legal weight as those from traditional providers. All our processes comply with relevant arbitration laws and regulations.

What types of disputes can Nomst handle?

We specialize in B2B contract disputes, SLA violations, payment disputes, and intellectual property conflicts common to small and medium businesses.

How does your AI ensure fairness?

Our AI only handles document analysis and case management. All decisions are made by qualified human arbitrators who are supported—not replaced—by our technology.

Can Nomst handle complex cases?

Absolutely. While our AI excels at streamlining simpler disputes, our expert arbitrators have decades of experience with complex commercial matters.
